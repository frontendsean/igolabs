Nerd Chair

Muuto

The Nerd chair by Berlin designer David Geckeler has a simplistic, but detail-orientated all-wood character. Whilst the appearance is unique, the overall material and craftsmanship all have classic Scandinavian design values. Two laminated plywood shells slot together to form the integration between seat and back without any metal fixings, giving the structure it’s organic shape. The Nerd chair (or Nørd chair) design won the Muuto Talent Award for Scandinavian Design Schools in 2011 and is now available in a natural oak or in a lacquered ash wood in grey, rose, petroleum, green, yellow, red or black.
